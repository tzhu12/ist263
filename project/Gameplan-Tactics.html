What information is underneath the plot:

This plot is a distribution of shots attempted by position for each team. According to the visualization of this barplot, it is clear to see that the difference of those 2 teams. On both guard position, the Warriors made more attempts than the Cavaliers did. This phenomenon is predictable since the Warriors have Splash Brothers: Stephen and Klay. Once you have the best and the second best shooters in NBA history, the only thing is designing tactics to make them shoot as many as possible. On both forward positions, the Cavaliers made more attempts than the Warriors did. There is no doubt that Lebron James takes a great portion of the small forward position. For Kevin Durant, he sacrificed a lot for this team. Back in times in Oklahoma Thunders, he averaged about 20 attempts per game. He is not the type of the player who impacts the game by putting the ball into the basket. On center’s position, both team don’t create shot opportunities for their center because of smaller lineups.

The Warrior’s tactic focus is slightly toward guards while the Cavaliers tactic focus is slightly to forwards.
